Astrophotography

Founded by passionate students, our club serves as the primary hub for astronomical activities at October STEM School. We bridge the gap between theoretical physics taught in class and practical, real-world observation.

  • Practical Experience: Regular telescope observation sessions tracking planets, moons, and deep-sky objects.
  • Scientific Method: Applying physics and math to predict and track celestial events, including eclipses and precise crescent sightings.
  • Community Outreach: Organizing events to educate the student body and local community about the wonders of space.
