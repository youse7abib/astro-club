Who We Are

Look Up. Learning Together.

OCT Astronomy Club is the official student astronomy organization at October STEM School, bringing together students with a shared passion for astronomy, astrophysics, and space science. Through lectures, sessions, astrophotography workshops, competitions, and outreach activities, we provide opportunities to explore the universe beyond the classroom.

Astrophotography

Founded by passionate students, our club serves as the primary hub for astronomical activities at October STEM School. We bridge the gap between theoretical physics taught in class and practical, real-world observation.

  • Hands-On Experience: Participate in observation sessions, astrophotography workshops, and practical activities that turn theory into experience.
  • Scientific Exploration: Learn the principles behind celestial phenomena and apply physics and mathematics to understand the universe.
  • Community Competitions: Take part in astronomy competitions, educational events, and outreach initiatives while connecting with fellow students who share a passion for space.
