Who we are

The Official Astronomy Club of October STEM School

As a student-run club affiliated with STEM High School for Boys – 6th of October, we provide physics training, host competitions, and publish academic materials for high schoolers from all around the world.

Specializations

Our Branches

Dive deep into a specific area of astronomical science with one of our specialized sub-groups.

01

Observational Astronomy

Learn practical sky observation, telescope operation, constellation identification, and observing planets, galaxies, nebulae, and deep-sky objects.

02

Astrophysics

Explore the physics of stars, black holes, galaxies, and the fundamental forces that shape the universe through modern astronomical research.

03

Cosmology

Study the origin, evolution, and large-scale structure of the universe, including the Big Bang, dark matter, dark energy, and cosmic expansion.

04

Planetology

Discover the formation, geology, atmospheres, and potential habitability of planets, moons, asteroids, and other bodies across our Solar System and beyond.
